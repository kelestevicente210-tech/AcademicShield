The canopy

Five personas, like five layers of canopy light

Each persona filters questions differently — from gentle morning light to the harsh glare that finds every gap. Choose one, or combine two for a realistic panel dynamic.

Layer 01

Supportive Mentor

Encouraging and constructive. Probing but fair questions.

Early practice, building confidence
Layer 02

Methodologist

Focuses exclusively on research design, sampling, and validity.

Students weak on methodology
Layer 03

Literature Expert

Challenges gaps in literature review and theoretical framework.

Students weak on related literature
Layer 04

Devil's Advocate

Takes the opposing position on your core argument. Challenges every assumption.

Late-stage stress testing
Layer 05 · Pro+

Expert Tutor

All of the above, plus pauses to explain why a question was asked and what a strong answer looks like.
